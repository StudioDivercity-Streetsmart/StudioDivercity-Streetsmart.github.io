Our Vision

Every street is a set of political choices. StreetSmart exists to make those choices visible to the people who live with them and to the people with the power to change them.

Why StreetSmart?

Your street shapes more than you might think. It affects how hot it gets in summer, where rainwater goes, whether children can move around independently, and whether walking, wheeling or waiting feels safe and comfortable.

Yet most streets are still designed mainly around traffic and parking. That choice affects daily life in ways we've largely stopped noticing.

Most people sense when something is wrong with their street. They just don't have the language or the data to name it. StreetSmart gives them both.

By combining street photos with lived experience, we help people read what their street is really built for, and build the evidence cities need before they design, invest or decide.

Meet the people behind StreetSmart

Reena Mahajan

Founder & CEO

Reena is an urban planner, architect and founder of Studio DiverCity, an independent urban design consultancy, with over 20 years of experience across Europe, Latin America, South Asia and Africa. While raising a child in Montevideo, the everyday limits of car-dominated streets became impossible to ignore. She ran the #MontevideoPacificada campaign, pushing for change until it came: safer crossings, new bike lanes, and a shift in how the city talked about its streets. Now based in Paris, she is building StreetSmart from the same conviction: that people should not have to be planners to understand, question and change the streets they live with every day.

Steven Cramer

Founding Fractional CTO

Steven is a technology and operations leader with nearly a decade at a large e-commerce company and an MBA from Chicago Booth. A dedicated pedestrian, cyclist, and hiker, he joined StreetSmart out of a conviction that streets should be built for people, not just cars. He designed and built StreetSmart's initial AI platform, and now serves as an advisor.
